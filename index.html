May The Front Be With You!

Michal Figiel

I`m Michal Figiel, and I`m a material science engineer


Science was always more than information I had to remember. Learning something new or practicing knowledge in live action is what drives me. At the beginning of my professional life, I was involved in metalworking due to my vocational education. The machines I operated used CNC control

Due to the job market situation in the 90s and the intense recruitment for compulsory military service, I continued my education while simultaneously working in the home renovations industry. After my studies, I took a job in the refrigeration industry as a technical and sales advisor..

I had to emigrate...

In 2004 I emigrated to Ireland, where for the 8 years I had my own bathroom refurbishment company. When I returned to Poland I started working as Office Manager for thermo solar company which lead me to becoming a Sales Manager for British heat exchanger company. From 2013 I was responsible for Quality Management at factory manufacturing architectural lighting.

Unfortunately for me, Poland was not ready to follow in the footsteps of countries like Norway or the Netherlands, and soon I realized that it is very difficult to sell in such an environment, so I returned to the building industry.
Since 2021, I have been based in Denmark, but the outcome of this endeavor remains to be seen.
